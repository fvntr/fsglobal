Our Work

Three Dimensions of Global Capital Movement

We organize across interconnected dimensions to create a counterweight to extractive capital narratives and build lasting infrastructure for community-centered finance across Africa, South Asia, Latin America, and beyond.

Indigenous & Land-Based Strategies

Weaving Indigenous-led capital and governance models across continents. Supporting self-determined economic futures rooted in traditional knowledge, sovereignty, and land stewardship.

Africa & Global Black Diaspora

Centering the African continent while mapping intersections across the diaspora. Building connections between Black-led movements in Africa, the Americas, Caribbean, and Europe.

Global South Development

Engaging with global development actors to redirect resources toward community-centered solutions across the Global South—away from extractive models.

Strategic Partners

We collaborate with aligned organizations to ensure capital strategies are grounded in community realities and movement infrastructure.

Buen Vivir Institute

LATAM anchor serving frontline social enterprises and NGOs with blended capital, matchmaking, and holistic support for community well-being.

Forward Global

Organizing new tech wealth toward impact, providing intervention opportunities upstream and countering extractive capital logic at the source.

Opportunity Collaboration

Global connection engine bringing together allocators and practitioners working on poverty alleviation and sustainable development.

Francesco Collaborative

Values-aligned network rooted in moral economy principles, engaging Catholic and interfaith capital toward justice and human dignity.

Kinflow

Collaborative of wealth navigators, legal strategists, spiritual guides, and movement advisors walking with capital stewards toward alignment.

Nexus

Global network of next-generation wealth holders committed to social change, providing pathways for young inheritors to align capital with values.
